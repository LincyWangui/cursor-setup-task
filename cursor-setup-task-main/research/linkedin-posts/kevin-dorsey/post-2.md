## Content
"Make more dials" is not a coaching conversation.

It's a wish dressed up as feedback.

And reps hear this and things like it every single day.

'Make more dials'
'Make more touches'
'Ask better questions'
'Find the pain!'
'Provide value first!'

Here's the problem:

"Make more dials" (and all the others listed and more) is the what.

Not the how.

Ok leaders talk about the what
Good leaders connect it to a why
Great leader help craft the how. 


## Key Insight
Effective outbound is built on structured, repeatable systems that enable teams to scale performance rather than relying on increased activity alone.
