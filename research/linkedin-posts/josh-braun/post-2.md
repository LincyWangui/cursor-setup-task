## Content
This made me better at sales.

Perhaps it will make you better too.

My success depended on how many uncomfortable conversations I had with the right people.

There were times when I didn’t make many cold calls.

I hid behind emails.

I ended up not doing well.

When I picked up the phone consistently, I had more uncomfortable conversations with my ideal clients.

The more conversations I had, the more money I made.

Your success depends on how many uncomfortable conversations you’re willing to have.

The phone isn’t a cactus.

Pick it up.

## Key Insight
Effective cold outreach mirrors the prospect’s context and priorities, making the message feel relevant and human rather than transactional.
