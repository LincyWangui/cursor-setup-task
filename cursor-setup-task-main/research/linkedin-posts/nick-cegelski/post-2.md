## Content
The biggest mistake in cold outreach is talking too much about your product.

Prospects don’t care about features—they care about outcomes. If your message doesn’t quickly show value or relevance, it gets ignored.

Effective outreach focuses on the prospect’s problem, not your solution.

## Key Insight
Cold emails perform better when they focus on the prospect’s outcomes rather than product features, making the message immediately relevant and valuable.
