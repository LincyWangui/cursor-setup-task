## Content
Today, many are not afraid of spending too little and getting too little.

They are afraid of spending meaningful budget in the wrong place.

That changes everything.

It means they are not optimizing for maximum value.

They are optimizing for minimum risk.

In many cases, I was trying to solve too many problems at once, adding more value, more capability, more upside, and pricing it fairly.

But that is not always what customers want.

What many actually want is a more affordable way to solve the most important problems within the budget they are already comfortable approving.

That has been one of my biggest lessons from Q1 2026:

A more affordable offer with decent value will often outsell a premium offer with outstanding value.
