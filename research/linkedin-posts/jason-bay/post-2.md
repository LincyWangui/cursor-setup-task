## Content
AI agents will be a net-negative on the buying experience. I'm calling it now.

Exactly like sequences:

- mid 2010s: "Holy sh*t an email from a stranger! This email had my name and company name in it!"
- late 2010s: "Half my inbox is filled with spammy sales emails"
- Today: "I never respond to sales emails"

AI agents will follow a similar, but accelerated journey:

- 2026: "Holy sh*t! I didn't have to interact with a rep. Awesome!"
- 2027: "I can't visit a website without getting spammed by AI."
- 2028: "Can I just talk to a human already?"

To be clear, AI agents will do wonders for existing clients. You'll be able to customize the platform without help, get answers to questions immediately, reduce manual work, etc.

But these AI agents ain't making the buyer process better for any serious B2B purchase in the mid-market or enterprise.

## Key Insight
Cold outreach improves when the focus shifts from pitching to creating curiosity. Using approaches that lower resistance—such as asking questions or offering perspective—helps start conversations instead of triggering immediate rejection.
