## Transcript
A key difference between effective and spam-like cold emails is personalization. Sending relevant messages to prospects who are likely to benefit from your offer increases response rates and reduces spam complaints.

A strong cold email typically includes:
- Subject line
- Personalized first line
- Compliment or observation
- Case study or proof
- Call to action
- Email signature

Instead of writing every email from scratch, using a structured template and customizing the first line for each prospect can significantly improve performance. Personalized first lines can make emails perform up to 10x better than generic templates.

## Key Insight
Cold email performance improves when using structured templates combined with targeted personalization, particularly in the opening line, which determines whether the email feels relevant or ignored.
