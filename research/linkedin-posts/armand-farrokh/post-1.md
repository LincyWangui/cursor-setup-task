## Content
In a cold call, your tone matters far more than the words you say.

Top performers focus on building confidence and authenticity during calls. This includes using talk tracks that feel natural, staying physically engaged (such as standing or moving), and creating an environment where rejection feels less personal.

They also focus on being human rather than perfect—embracing mistakes, responding naturally, and even using humor when things go wrong.

The goal of a cold call is not just to book a meeting, but to break through corporate formality and create a genuine human interaction.

## Key Insight
Cold outreach becomes more effective when it prioritizes authenticity and human connection over perfection, making conversations feel natural and engaging rather than scripted.
