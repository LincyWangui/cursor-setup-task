## Content
Cold call openers I have used and loved:

"Hey Jimmy, it's Jc with Gong, can I have like 5 seconds to explain why I am bugging you?"

"Hey Sally, it's Jc with Gong, how've ya been?"

"Hey Santiago, its Jc with Gong, did I catch you at the worst time in human history?"

"Hey Georgy, this is Jc calling with Gong.io, this is a cold call but it is extremely well researched and hyper-specific to you, can I have like 10 seconds to explain why I am calling?"

All of them have led to meetings being booked. 

All of them have led to getting hung up on.

There is no magic recipe for a cold call. 

But there is one thing that will stop even the highest converting reps from succeeding. 

And that is not picking up the phone and making the dial.

So don't overthink it.

Make the call.


## Key Insight
Cold outreach success depends on strong openings that feel natural and conversational, reducing resistance and increasing the chances of engagement.
