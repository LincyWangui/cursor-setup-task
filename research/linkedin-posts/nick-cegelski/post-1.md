## Content
Most cold emails fail before they’re even opened.

If your subject line doesn’t feel relevant or specific, it gets ignored instantly. Generic subject lines signal low effort and reduce the chances of engagement.

The best performing subject lines are simple, clear, and directly tied to the recipient’s role or priorities.

## Key Insight
Cold outreach effectiveness starts before the email is opened. Clear and relevant subject lines significantly increase the chances of engagement.
